Figure 24c.7 Relationship between the estimated prevalence of inadequate zinc intake and the prevalence of childhood stunting. Stunting data (low height-for-age) are for children less than five years of age in138 low- and middle-income countries. The solid line represents the line of identity (intercept = 0, slope = 1). The dashed line represents the best-fit regression line. Dotted lines demarcate countries with a high risk of inadequate zinc intake and where the prevalence of stunting is > 20% From Wessells KR, Brown KH. PLoS One. 2012;7(11):e50568.
